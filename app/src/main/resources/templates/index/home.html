Welcome to Primaza

To allow to bind a microservice/application with a service (Database, Broker, NoSQL, etc), different steps are needed. As this PoC do not yet integrate all the systems which should be part of the final architecture, we will have to create some data manually. The process to follow is pretty foward and can be summarized in a few steps:
- Register a service
- Wait till the service(s) will be discovered on the clusters and will become available
- Create the service's credential
- Populate a claim to request to bind a service
- Select the deployment where the secret will be injected/mounted
Enjoy !!
